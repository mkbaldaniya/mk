Branding Reimagined

This project focused on a complete brand identity overhaul for a burgeoning startup. The goal was to develop a distinctive and memorable brand presence that resonated with their target audience.

What I Did:

  • Developed a new, modern logo from concept to final design
  • Created a comprehensive brand style guide (color palette, typography, imagery)
  • Designed various brand assets, including business cards, social media templates, and website elements
  • Ensured brand consistency across all touchpoints

Tools Used:

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Figma.

Impact:

The new branding significantly improved the startup's market perception and helped them establish a strong, professional image, leading to increased customer engagement.
